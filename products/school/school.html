School/College/Coaching Management System

Educational Institute Management System is a complete solution for managing a school/college/coaching/Institute/University. In other words, an enhanced Software that assists in organizing the day-to-day activities of an institute By this Education ERP, you can manage entire end-to-end operations with a flexible & powerful business application that integrates core Educational Operation like Admission, Academic System, Exam management, Student Panel, Teacher Panel and much more along with the accounting, financials, HRM, inventory management.

  • Panel, Student, Parents & Teacher Management Panel, Account

  • Management Panel, Attendance with Auto SMS Panel, Library Panel

  • School Administration

  • HR Management

  • Online Student Status

  • All Financial Report

  • Real-Time Notification for Student & Teacher (In, Out & Absent)

How Does School/College/Coaching Management System helps you?

  • Admin can create Notices via SMS (Massage)

  • Parents known his son or daughter attendance via auto SMS/Massage for school In

  • Out & Absent by finger punch/RFID Card

  • Android App for every panel, get access to information easily

  • Continuous assessment facility

  • Admin can create and it will auto appear to the website
